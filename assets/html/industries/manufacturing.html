Manufacturing involves creating value by transforming some form of raw materials into finished goods using tools, technology, labor, and machinery. Some of the most important manufacturing companies are those involved in producing aircrafts, engines, automobiles, computers, electronics, furniture, machinery, ships, steel, etc. Manufacturing can range from commodities to something as specialised as a Rolls Royce.


Manufacturing companies witness constant challenges in forecasting the demand for their products, ensuring optimal inventory levels, enhancing process efficiency, and keeping up with the new technologies and alternatives. The fourth industrial revolution (industry 4.0) has brought new technologies on the horizon. These include leveraging Internet of Things (IoT), robotics, and artificial intelligence to enhance efficiency and quality of products.


COVID -19 has further disrupted the industry with newer challenges requiring a complete move to digital which requires digital transformation in all divisions of the business and ensuring successful change management.


Moreover, Manufacturing industry constantly requires consumer insights based on behavior changes, trend changes and also the changes in the modes of buying. These trends evolve every season and every year, impacting the demand and profitability.


We assist manufacturing companies with consumer insights and analytics to understand and adapt to the changes in demand and consumer profile.


We also help in digital transformation of businesses, including strategizing, developing the implementation plans and ensuring change management.
