Retail

We help our Consulting and Research clients by extending highly focused and specialised research and analytics for their clients’ industries.

BW builds custom solutions to help retailers across all sectors—from grocery, luxury and apparel to restaurants, travel & leisure and more. We develop and implement tailored strategies that address all the challenges and opportunities in retail - digital disruption, new business models, increasing consumer expectations and evolving consumer behavior.


We partner with clients to explore the opportunities on new platforms, develop the most optimum multichannel strategies, and improve the overall digital performance.


We also provide advanced analytics to help identify data-driven improvement opportunities in the business offering, pricing, product bundling, and user experience.


Case: BW worked with a leading US-based healthcare brand launching a startup in the moms wellness niche. We helped the startup with its entire business planning - which areas to play in, what products to sell, how and where to market the products, and how to bring awareness to the prospective customers. What sets us apart in all our consulting engagements is that we also help our clients implement the recommended strategies. We helped the startup in setting up their social media presence, create analytics on the products and pricing, and also helped them in the branding and messaging apt for nudging the prospective clients.
