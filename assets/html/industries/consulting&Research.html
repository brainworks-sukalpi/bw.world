Consulting & Research

Consulting and Research sector involves the research of existing knowledge and deriving the relevant insights from the research. It further includes working in collaboration with the client to provide an external, independent, and objective perspective on the client's issues, and provide appropriate recommendations on the basis of a diagnosis. The consulting and research companies have expertise in their own specific areas like digital marketing, education, and public policies.

Consulting and Research companies today face the challenge of Big Data, i.e., the breadth of information available online and the concerns regarding its reliability and applicability. There is also a growing need of specialist knowledge as opposed to broad knowledge which needs expertise in certain focused areas. There is a growing need to derive just the right insights based on highly reliable and accurate data.
