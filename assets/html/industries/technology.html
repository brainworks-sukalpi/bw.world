Technology

Helping the disruptors disrupt effectively

The technology sector consists of companies and startups doing research and development or distribution of tech-based goods and services. Key businesses revolve around the production of software, computers, or products and services relating to information technology. The sector offers a wide arrange of products and services for both customers and other businesses.

01

B2C

Personal computers, mobile devices, wearable technology, home appliances and consumer software

02

B2B

Enterprise software, logistics systems, data security solutions.

Some of the important technology trends shaping the industry include Applied AI, Robotic Process Automation, Blockchain, Big data and analytics, and virtualization.


While technology is the main driver of disruption across industries today, the path to success for a tech company is laden with challenges. Getting the right talent, creating a successful business and monetization model, marketing the offering, managing finances, the fierce competition in the space and managing the various stakeholders in the ecosystem are some of the key ones.


We work with several tech startups led by geeks and engineers on the verge of disrupting the industry with their innovation. We help them with converting their idea and prototype into a viable business model, raise money, market their offering, build an ecosystem around it, manage finances, build a team.
