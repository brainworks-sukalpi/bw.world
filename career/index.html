Work with Us @ Brainworks

The Hawaiian islands in the US are a pristine tourist destination with about a million of them arriving there every year. Two young entrepreneurs from the city of Hilo in Hawaii observe that there are no Hawaiian-themed bars or restaurants in their city. Besides, most restaurants run on low profitability as wastages are extremely high. They envision a beautiful experience with authentic Hawaiian cuisine, Tiki totems, an open air, roof-top bar, a pool, trellis with local vine plants and what not.

They’ve got the creative vision but not the business skillset to plan and execute. From studying the market, working out the finances, building an operations plan, raising money, to branding and marketing their restaurant, they need all of that figured out.

Could you help them? Does this excite you?

Working with us @ Brainworks, you’ll be expanding your comfort zone constantly. You’ll be exploring new areas of business and knowledge, learning new skills on the go, and doing exciting work that makes an impact.

Whether you’re an experienced professional, a recent graduate, or still in college, if you’re obsessed about diagnosing and solving real-world problems and capturing opportunities of growth for your clients, Brainworks will offer you the avenues.
